"I have always tried to compensate with dedication and hard work for what I lack in natural talent." 


I am a survivor. This is how I can describe myself in just a few words. I always work very hard for what I want and that is what is recommending me the most.

My story in a nutshell

I come from a small town in Romania. I studied at the Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences at the Babeș-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca. I lived for almost two years in London where I extended my knowledge in this domain. I came back to finish by studies. Now I am a student at the Faculty of Letters, Cluj-Napoca (the BA in Romanian and English Languages and Literatures) and at the Master's of Media Communication at the Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences.
Now I started thinking of this new prospect of working as a freelancer. Even if I am at the very beginning in this domain, I always work with full dedication. For me it is important to connect with each brand that I work with. Finding the right designs is a process that implies a lot of work, dedication, imagination but also of understanding the souls of those brands.

My Timeline

 2015-2017 

Studied English Journalism at the Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences, Babeș-Bolyai University,
Cluj-Napoca

During this time, I started studying about graphic design, acquiring the basic knowledge in design tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Adobe InDesign

2017-2018

Digital Marketing Assistant at Signarama UK

Signarama UK is a signage company and it is part of the franchise called Signarama. Working for them for one year, I developed my skills regarding Photoshop and InDesign as I was the one in charge with designing their marketing materials. I also learnt how to work in other programmes of the Adobe Suite, especially Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Premiere. 
As I was the Digital Marketing Assitant, I started working with WordPress and also building their online presence through their social media pages.

2018-Present

Digital Marketing Assistant, Askham Village Community

Askham Village Community is a group of five homes that provides both care and rehabilitation services. Working for them for two years now, I got the chance to learn all about the Askham brand and the story behind it.
At Askham, I am not only dealing with the main marketing materials they need and with the blog posting, but I am also in charge of their online presence. With Askham I developed my skills in terms of WordPress, as I redesigned their main website, as well as creating one new website for their training centre, AVC Training. Today I am in charge of their three websites that they have, as well as of their marketing materials and of their online presence.

2019-2020

BA in English Journalism and started the Faculty of Letters

As I cam back to Romania, I rejoined the courses and I achieved the Bachelor's Degree in English Journalism. As well as that, I wanted to come back to a passion of mine (studying Romanian and English Literature), so I enrolled in the the Romanian-English Language and Literature specialisation from the Faculty of Letters.

2020-Present

Started my Master's studies in Media Communication

Following the same path, I am currently a MA student at the same Department of Journalism and Digital Media of the Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences.
