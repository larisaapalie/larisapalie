Graphic Design

Graphic Design is the domain in which I worked the most. From modifying pictures and creating videos to complex marketing materials such as leaflets, brochures, branded email campaigns and cards, the expertise that I gained over the years encourages me to say that if you want a person to work in accordance with your brand guidelines, I am that person.

Listening what you exactly need and the constant feedback will help in the creation of excellent marketing materials.

If you need a portfolio with some of my works that might interest you, please go to the Contact page, write me an email and I will send over to you all the materials that you think would be useful to help you come to a decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

I am not sure what you are offering. What can I do?                                 

Please check the Contact page, write me an email and let's discuss about your worries.

I have a website and a brand identity but I would like to freshen them up. Can you help me?                                   

Yes, sure. A bit of refreshing is sometimes necessary. Please write me an email (check out the Contact page) and then we will see what options we have.

I am a person who does not have much time and leaves things at the very last minute. Can you work under pressure?

Of course! Deadlines are the most important aspect of this job. I will make sure that your designs are ready on time and that they will also look great.

What programmes do you usually use?

I am using the Adobe Suite (for designing all the materials) and WordPress for web design. However, if you have some specific requirements, just contact me and we can work around your options.

Are you working with some kind of kits?

I do not usually work with kits since different companies have different needs. However, we can discuss about some kind of kits if you need assistance with more than just one of my services.

The prices are not visible because they depend on the complexity of the project. There are a lot of factors that influence the final price but after the initial discussion, the initial assessment will also contain the detailed prices so you will be able to know exactly the final price.
